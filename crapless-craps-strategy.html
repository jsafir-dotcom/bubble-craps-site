Best Crapless Craps Strategy

Crapless craps is more exciting than standard craps—but it also comes with worse odds. That means your strategy should focus on minimizing risk and avoiding the most dangerous bets.

Key Principle: Avoid Extreme Point Numbers

In crapless craps, numbers like 2, 3, 11, and 12 can become point numbers. These are extremely hard to roll again before a 7.

If one of these numbers becomes the point, your chances of winning drop significantly.

Stick to Simple Bets

The safest approach is to keep your strategy simple:

  • Use pass line bets
  • Avoid chasing losses
  • Limit exposure on risky points

Be Careful with Odds Bets

In regular craps, odds bets reduce the house edge. In crapless craps, they are still useful—but less effective due to the added point numbers.

Manage Your Bankroll

Because of higher volatility, it’s important to:

  • Set a budget before playing
  • Avoid increasing bets after losses
  • Play smaller amounts on high-risk points

Is There a Winning Strategy?

No strategy can eliminate the house edge in crapless craps. The best you can do is reduce risk and play in a disciplined way.

Best Way to Practice

The easiest way to improve is to practice without risking real money.
